Becoming a Personal Trainer

Personal training has always been the premium product in a gym environment.

At Balanced Training we educate our learners how to actually be functional in the fitness industry and are able to start their careers the moment they receive their certificate for the course.

The course is broken down into 2 parts initially the first being the Gym instructing course, this gives a great overview of the job role of the basic gym instructor.

The second part of the course is the personal trainer qualification this builds on the basics learnt on the gym instructor course.


Below there is more information on each of the different courses we offer:

Certificate in Gym Instructing

The course has 5 units which are:

  • Principles of anatomy, physiology and fitness
  • Professionalism and customer care for fitness instructors
  • Health and safety in the fitness environment
  • Conducting client consultations to support positive behaviour change
  • Planning and instructing gym-based exercise

    • Diploma in Personal Training

    The course has 6 units which are:

  • Applied anatomy and physiology for exercise, health and fitness
  • The principles of nutrition and their application to exercise and health
  • Understanding lifestyle, health, wellbeing and common medical conditions
  • Encouraging positive health and fitness behaviours in clients
  • Programme design and delivery for personal training
  • Professionalism and business acumen for personal trainers
